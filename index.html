About Us

At West Coast Euros, we're more than just an aftermarket parts store, we're a community built by enthusiasts, for enthusiasts.

What started as a passion for German performance cars has grown into a brand trusted by thousands of BMW, Audi, VW, and Porsche owners worldwide. From carbon fiber aero to lighting upgrades, intakes, and exhausts, we specialize in parts that transform your car while staying true to the lifestyle that unites us all.

But we're not just here to sell parts. Our builds, installs, and content are all about creating something bigger. We strive to bring the community together with our shop builds like the BMW 335i build series and our booths at events like Bimmer Invasion. We want to create a culture. One that pushes enthusiasts to make their build better. WCE is here to support you with quality parts, transparent service, and a community that has your back.

We believe cars bring people together, and that's exactly what West Coast Euros Racing Club is all about. From first-time modifiers to seasoned car builders, we're building a space where passion meets community.

This is more than a shop. This is West Coast Euros. Why blend in?
